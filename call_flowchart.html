General Rules — All Calls

  1. Do NOT sell on the phone. The call is for qualification ONLY. The real selling happens in chat (WhatsApp).
  2. Keep calls SHORT — 2–3 minutes max. The client wasn't expecting your call.
  3. Don't push. If a client isn't ready — that's perfectly normal. Log it and move on.
  4. Say "I", not "we." First person, personal, conversational.
  5. Log everything after each call. CRM entry: client group, interest level, next contact date, notes.
  6. If client is busy: "No problem — when would be a better time for you?" → log the callback time.
  7. If you get voicemail: Do NOT leave a voicemail. Send a reactivation message in WhatsApp instead (scripts [85] or [87]).
  8. WhatsApp is our preferred channel. Always suggest it first. Facebook Messenger is second, email is third.
  9. Tone: Confident, friendly, helpful — never pushy. Helpful advisor, not telemarketer.
  10. ALWAYS ask the client when THEY are ready — never impose your own timeline.
